Sambar(COMBO)

A traditional South Indian dish made with lentils and vegetables.

Price: ₹100

Ingredients:

  • Toor dal (pigeon pea lentils)
  • Tamarind
  • Mixed vegetables (carrots, drumsticks, beans, pumpkin)
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Sambar powder
  • Turmeric powder
  • Mustard seeds
  • Curry leaves
  • Dry red chilies
  • Asafoetida
  • Oil
  • Salt

Process:

  1. Cook Lentils: Cook toor dal with turmeric powder until soft and mash well.
  2. Prepare Tamarind Extract: Soak tamarind in water and extract the juice.
  3. Cook Vegetables: In a pot, cook mixed vegetables with water until tender.
  4. Add Dal and Spices: Add the cooked dal, tamarind extract, sambar powder, and salt to the vegetables. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Tempering: Heat oil, add mustard seeds, dry red chilies, curry leaves, and asafoetida. Pour over the sambar and mix well.

Rasam(COMBO)

A tangy and spicy South Indian soup made with tamarind and spices.

Price: ₹80

Ingredients:

  • Tamarind
  • Tomatoes
  • Rasam powder
  • Turmeric powder
  • Mustard seeds
  • Cumin seeds
  • Curry leaves
  • Dry red chilies
  • Asafoetida
  • Garlic
  • Coriander leaves
  • Oil
  • Salt

Process:

  1. Prepare Tamarind Extract: Soak tamarind in water and extract the juice.
  2. Cook Base: Boil tamarind extract with chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, rasam powder, and salt.
  3. Add Water: Add water to the mixture and bring to a boil. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Tempering: Heat oil, add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, dry red chilies, curry leaves, and asafoetida. Pour over the rasam and mix well.
  5. Garnish: Add crushed garlic and chopped coriander leaves before serving.

Kara Kulambu(COMBO)

A spicy and tangy South Indian curry made with tamarind and spices.

Price: ₹120

Ingredients:

  • Tamarind
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Brinjal (eggplant)
  • Lady's finger (okra)
  • Sambar powder
  • Turmeric powder
  • Mustard seeds
  • Fenugreek seeds
  • Curry leaves
  • Dry red chilies
  • Asafoetida
  • Oil
  • Salt

Process:

  1. Prepare Tamarind Extract: Soak tamarind in water and extract the juice.
  2. Fry Vegetables: Heat oil and fry brinjal and lady's finger until half-cooked.
  3. Cook Base: In a pot, sauté onions and tomatoes. Add turmeric powder, sambar powder, and salt. Add the tamarind extract and bring to a boil.
  4. Add Vegetables: Add the fried vegetables to the tamarind mixture and cook until tender.
  5. Tempering: Heat oil, add mustard seeds, fenugreek seeds, dry red chilies, curry leaves, and asafoetida. Pour over the kulambu and mix well.

Moru Kulambu(COMBO)

A traditional South Indian yogurt-based curry with spices.

Price: ₹90

Ingredients:

  • Yogurt
  • Turmeric powder
  • Chili powder
  • Mustard seeds
  • Cumin seeds
  • Fenugreek seeds
  • Curry leaves
  • Dry red chilies
  • Asafoetida
  • Green chilies
  • Coconut (optional)
  • Oil
  • Salt

Process:

  1. Prepare Yogurt Mixture: Whisk yogurt with turmeric powder, chili powder, and salt.
  2. Cook Base: Heat oil, add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, fenugreek seeds, dry red chilies, and curry leaves. Add asafoetida and green chilies.
  3. Add Yogurt: Lower the heat and add the whisked yogurt mixture. Stir continuously to prevent curdling.
  4. Add Coconut Paste (optional): Grind coconut with some water to make a paste and add to the mixture. Cook for a few more minutes.
  5. Simmer: Allow the mixture to simmer on low heat for 5-10 minutes. Do not boil.
