Porota(COMBO)

A flaky and layered South Indian flatbread.

Price: ₹50

Ingredients:

  • All-purpose flour (maida)
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Oil

Process:

  1. Prepare Dough: Mix all-purpose flour, salt, and water to make a soft dough. Rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Roll and Layer: Divide the dough into balls, roll into thin circles, and brush with oil. Fold into pleats and roll into a spiral.
  3. Cook: Heat a griddle (tawa), place the rolled dough, and cook both sides until golden brown, brushing with oil.
  4. Serve: Serve hot with curry or salna.

Salna(COMBO)

A spicy and flavorful South Indian curry served with porota or parotta.

Price: ₹80

Ingredients:

  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Coconut milk
  • Curry masala powder
  • Fennel seeds
  • Cloves
  • Cinnamon stick
  • Bay leaves
  • Green chilies
  • Coriander leaves
  • Oil
  • Salt

Process:

  1. Sauté Ingredients: Sauté onions, tomatoes, green chilies, and spices in oil until soft.
  2. Add Coconut Milk: Add coconut milk and curry masala powder. Simmer until flavors meld.
  3. Adjust Seasoning: Adjust salt and spices according to taste.
  4. Serve: Serve hot with porota or parotta.

Poori Masala(COMBO)

A classic South Indian dish of spiced potato curry served with poori.

Price: ₹70

Ingredients:

  • Potatoes
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Green chilies
  • Ginger
  • Curry leaves
  • Mustard seeds
  • Urad dal (black gram)
  • Turmeric powder
  • Red chili powder
  • Coriander powder
  • Garam masala powder
  • Oil
  • Salt

Process:

  1. Cook Potatoes: Boil potatoes until soft, peel, and mash coarsely.
  2. Sauté Ingredients: Sauté onions, tomatoes, green chilies, ginger, and spices in oil until onions are translucent.
  3. Add Potatoes: Add mashed potatoes and cook until well combined with the spices.
  4. Adjust Seasoning: Adjust salt and spices according to taste.
  5. Serve: Serve hot with poori.

Alu Porata(COMBO)

A delicious South Indian stuffed potato flatbread.

Price: ₹60

Ingredients:

  • Wheat flour
  • Potatoes
  • Onions
  • Green chilies
  • Ginger
  • Coriander leaves
  • Cumin seeds
  • Turmeric powder
  • Red chili powder
  • Garam masala powder
  • Oil
  • Salt

Process:

  1. Prepare Dough: Mix wheat flour, salt, and water to make a soft dough. Rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Prepare Filling: Boil potatoes until soft, peel, and mash. Sauté with onions, green chilies, ginger, and spices.
  3. Roll and Stuff: Divide dough into balls, roll into circles, place filling in the center, seal, and roll into a flatbread.
  4. Cook: Heat a griddle (tawa), place the stuffed flatbread, and cook both sides until golden brown, brushing with oil.
  5. Serve: Serve hot with yogurt or chutney.
