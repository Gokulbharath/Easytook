Chicken Biryani(COMBO)

Delicious biryani made with fragrant rice, spices, and tender meat.

Price: ₹200

Ingredients:

  • Basmati rice
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Yogurt
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Mint leaves
  • Coriander leaves
  • Green chilies
  • Red chili powder
  • Turmeric powder
  • Garam masala powder
  • Bay leaves
  • Cloves
  • Cardamom pods
  • Cinnamon stick
  • Ghee
  • Salt

Process:

  1. Marinate Chicken: Mix chicken pieces with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Marinate for at least 1 hour.
  2. Cook Rice: Boil the rice with bay leaves, cloves, cardamom, and cinnamon stick until 70% cooked. Drain and set aside.
  3. Fry Onions: Heat ghee in a large pot. Add sliced onions and fry until golden brown. Remove half of the fried onions and set aside.
  4. Prepare Chicken: In the same pot, add marinated chicken, chopped tomatoes, green chilies, mint, and coriander leaves. Cook until the chicken is tender.
  5. Layer and Cook: Layer the partially cooked rice over the chicken mixture. Top with the reserved fried onions, sprinkle garam masala powder, and drizzle some ghee. Cover tightly and cook on low heat (dum) for 20-25 minutes.

Vegetable Biryani(COMBO)

Delightful vegetable biryani with mixed vegetables and aromatic spices.

Price: ₹180

Ingredients:

  • Mixed vegetables (carrots, beans, peas, potatoes, cauliflower)
  • Basmati rice
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Yogurt
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Mint leaves
  • Coriander leaves
  • Green chilies

  • Cloves
  • Cardamom pods

  • Salt

Process:
