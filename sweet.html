Laddu(COMBO)

A popular Indian sweet made from gram flour and sugar.

Price: ₹20 per piece

Ingredients:

  • Besan (gram flour)
  • Ghee (clarified butter)
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom powder
  • Nuts (optional)

Process:

  1. Roast Besan: Roast besan in ghee until golden brown and aromatic.
  2. Add Sugar: Mix roasted besan with powdered sugar and cardamom powder.
  3. Shape: Shape into round balls (laddus) while still warm.
  4. Optional: Garnish with nuts if desired.
  5. Serve: Serve at room temperature.

Jalebi(COMBO)

A crispy and syrupy Indian sweet made from fermented batter.

Price: ₹30 per piece

Ingredients:

  • All-purpose flour (maida)
  • Yogurt
  • Sugar
  • Saffron strands
  • Cardamom powder
  • Oil (for frying)

Process:

  1. Prepare Batter: Ferment maida and yogurt batter for a few hours.
  2. Make Sugar Syrup: Boil sugar with water until syrupy consistency, add saffron and cardamom powder.
  3. Fry Jalebis: Heat oil, pour batter in concentric circles, fry until crisp and golden.
  4. Dip in Syrup: Dip fried jalebis in warm sugar syrup for a few seconds.
  5. Serve: Serve hot or warm.

Gulab Jamun(COMBO)

A classic Indian dessert made from khoya and soaked in sugar syrup.

Price: ₹25 per piece

Ingredients:

  • Khoya (milk solids)
  • All-purpose flour (maida)
  • Milk
  • Ghee (clarified butter)
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom powder
  • Rose water

Process:

  1. Prepare Dough: Mix khoya, maida, and milk to make a soft dough.
  2. Shape: Shape into small balls (jamuns).
  3. Fry: Deep fry in ghee until golden brown.
  4. Soak: Soak fried jamuns in sugar syrup flavored with cardamom and rose water.
  5. Serve: Serve warm or at room temperature.

Mysore Pak(COMBO)

A rich and melt-in-mouth Indian sweet made from gram flour, ghee, and sugar.

Price: ₹40 per piece

Ingredients:

  • Besan (gram flour)
  • Ghee (clarified butter)
  • Sugar
  • Water

Process:

  1. Prepare Sugar Syrup: Boil sugar with water until one-string consistency.
  2. Mix Ingredients: Mix besan and ghee in a separate pan.
  3. Cook: Cook besan-ghee mixture on low heat until aromatic and golden brown.
  4. Add Syrup: Add hot sugar syrup gradually while stirring continuously.
  5. Set: Pour mixture into a greased tray, let it cool, and cut into pieces.
  6. Serve: Serve at room temperature.

Palkova(COMBO)

A creamy and rich Indian milk sweet made from milk and sugar.

Price: ₹50 per piece

Ingredients:

  • Milk
  • Sugar

Process:

  1. Reduce Milk: Boil milk until it reduces to a thick consistency.
  2. Add Sugar: Add sugar and continue cooking until it thickens further.
  3. Cool: Let it cool and set in a greased tray.
  4. Set: Cut into desired shapes and serve.
  5. Serve: Serve at room temperature.
