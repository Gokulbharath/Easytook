Chicken Gravy(COMBO)

A rich and flavorful chicken curry.

Price: ₹180

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Green chilies
  • Coriander powder
  • Cumin powder
  • Turmeric powder
  • Red chili powder
  • Garam masala powder
  • Curry leaves
  • Coconut milk
  • Oil
  • Salt

Process:

  1. Sauté Ingredients: Sauté onions, green chilies, and ginger-garlic paste in oil until golden brown.
  2. Add Spices: Add coriander powder, cumin powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and garam masala powder. Sauté until aromatic.
  3. Cook Chicken: Add chicken pieces, tomatoes, and curry leaves. Cook until chicken is tender.
  4. Add Coconut Milk: Pour coconut milk and simmer until gravy thickens.
  5. Adjust Seasoning: Adjust salt and spices according to taste.
  6. Serve: Serve hot with rice or bread.

Fish Kulambu(COMBO)

A tangy and spicy South Indian fish curry.

Price: ₹200

Ingredients:

  • Fish (preferably pomfret or kingfish)
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Tamarind
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Green chilies
  • Coriander powder
  • Cumin powder
  • Turmeric powder
  • Red chili powder
  • Mustard seeds
  • Fenugreek seeds
  • Curry leaves
  • Coconut milk
  • Oil
  • Salt

Process:

  1. Prepare Tamarind Extract: Soak tamarind in water and extract the juice.
  2. Sauté Ingredients: Sauté onions, green chilies, and ginger-garlic paste in oil until golden brown.
  3. Add Spices: Add coriander powder, cumin powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Sauté until aromatic.
  4. Add Fish: Add fish pieces and tomatoes. Cook for a few minutes.
  5. Add Tamarind Extract: Pour tamarind extract and simmer until fish is cooked and gravy thickens.
  6. Tempering: Heat oil, add mustard seeds, fenugreek seeds, and curry leaves. Pour over the kulambu and mix well.
  7. Serve: Serve hot with rice.

Chicken 65(COMBO)

A spicy and crispy South Indian chicken starter.

Price: ₹150

Ingredients:

  • Chicken (boneless)
  • Curd (yogurt)
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Corn flour
  • Red chili powder
  • Turmeric powder
  • Garam masala powder
  • Curry leaves
  • Green chilies
  • Coriander leaves
  • Oil
  • Salt

Process:

  1. Marinate Chicken: Marinate chicken pieces with curd, ginger-garlic paste, corn flour, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala powder, and salt. Rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Fry Chicken: Heat oil, fry marinated chicken until crispy and golden brown.
  3. Temper: Heat oil, add curry leaves, green chilies, and toss fried chicken pieces in it.
  4. Garnish: Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.

Chicken Soup(COMBO)

A comforting and nourishing chicken soup.

Price: ₹100

Ingredients:

  • Chicken (with bones)
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Bay leaves
  • Black peppercorns
  • Coriander leaves
  • Salt

Process:

  1. Prepare Broth: Boil chicken with onions, garlic, ginger, bay leaves, and black peppercorns until chicken is cooked and flavorful broth is obtained.
  2. Add Vegetables: Add diced carrots, celery, and cook until vegetables are tender.
  3. Season: Season with salt and pepper as per taste.
  4. Serve: Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot.
